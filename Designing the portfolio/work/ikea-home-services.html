IKEA Home Services

Peer‑to‑peer home improvement marketplace

Connecting homeowners with verified local professionals across 50+ categories.

Launched Madrid 2024, now live in seven Spanish cities. 500,000+ downloads, 21,000 service requests, 4.9/5 satisfaction.

Client: IKEA Role: Senior Product Designer Period: 2023 — Present Scope: Product Design · Marketplace · Mobile

Context

Marketplace for home services

IKEA wanted to extend its ecosystem beyond furniture, offering a curated platform where homeowners could book trusted professionals for repairs, installations, and improvements.

Challenge

Build trust and scale quickly

Launching a service marketplace required vetting professionals, ensuring consistent quality, and handling payments across multiple jurisdictions.

My Role

End‑to‑end product design

I led UX research, interaction design, and visual design, collaborating with product owners, engineers, and in‑store research teams.

Approach

Iterative, data‑driven design

We prototyped booking flows, ran early usability tests in IKEA stores, and refined the experience based on conversion metrics.

Execution

From concept to launch

  • Design system integration with existing IKEA brand assets.
  • Responsive mobile‑first UI, compatible with iOS and Android.
  • Accessibility audit achieving WCAG AA compliance.

Outcomes

Strong adoption and satisfaction

  • 500,000+ downloads within the first year.
  • 21,000 service requests processed.
  • Average rating 4.9/5 from users.

Learnings

Balancing scale with quality

Ensuring consistent vetting while scaling across regions required a robust partner‑onboarding workflow that we codified into the product.
