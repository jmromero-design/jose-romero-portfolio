Meliá Hotels International

Campaign Management Platform

Product Design · Web Application · Hospitality

  • Client: Meliá Hotels International
  • Role: Senior Product Designer
  • Period: 2022 – 2024
  • Scope: End‑to‑end platform design, research, UI, handoff

Context

Why a new platform?

Meliá needed a centralised tool to plan, launch, and analyse marketing campaigns across 30+ hotels worldwide. The existing workflow relied on spreadsheets and fragmented tools, leading to inconsistencies and missed optimisation opportunities.

Challenge

Complex stakeholder ecosystem

Multiple regional marketing teams, brand managers, and senior executives required tailored views while maintaining a single source of truth. Balancing flexibility with governance was critical.

My Role

Design lead

Led workshops with marketing directors, defined information architecture, created high‑fidelity prototypes, and collaborated with engineers for implementation.

Approach

Human‑centred and data‑driven

Conducted user interviews, built journey maps, and ran rapid‑iteration tests. Employed a modular UI system to allow regional customisation while preserving global brand consistency.

Execution

From prototype to production

Delivered a responsive web app built with React, integrated with Meliá's internal API layer. Added analytics dashboards, role‑based permissions, and export capabilities.

Outcomes

Impact on the business

  • 30 % reduction in campaign launch time
  • Improved reporting accuracy, leading to a 12 % lift in ROI on campaigns
  • Positive feedback from all regional teams on usability and data visibility

Learnings

Key takeaways

Balancing global governance with regional autonomy required a robust permission model and clear UI patterns. Early stakeholder alignment and iterative validation were essential for adoption.
