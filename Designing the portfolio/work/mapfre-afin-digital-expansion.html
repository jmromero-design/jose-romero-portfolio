Mapfre — AFIN App & Digital Expansion

Self‑service financial app + cross‑border platform scaling

Product Design · Mobile · FinTech

Designed the AFIN app for Mapfre’s retail investors and led the digital expansion to Italy, Germany and Spain, aligning UX across markets while complying with local regulations.

Client: Mapfre Role: Senior Product Designer Period: 2022 — 2024 Scope: Product Design · Mobile · FinTech · International Expansion

Context

Building a self‑service investment platform

Mapfre needed a modern, user‑friendly mobile app (AFIN) that allowed retail customers to manage investments, view portfolio performance, and execute transactions, while also providing a web‑based platform for international markets.

Challenge

Regulatory diversity and cross‑market consistency

Each European market (Spain, Italy, Germany) has distinct financial regulations, language requirements, and user expectations. The design had to be adaptable without fragmenting the brand experience.

My Role

End‑to‑end product design leadership

Conducted user research, defined the information architecture, created high‑fidelity UI kits, and collaborated with engineering to implement a modular, localisation‑ready design system.

Approach

Research‑first, modular UI

Ran interviews with existing Mapfre clients, mapped financial journeys, and established a component library that could be tweaked per market while preserving core branding.

Execution

From prototype to production rollout

  • Responsive mobile UI built with React Native for iOS & Android.
  • Web dashboard built with React, sharing the same component library.
  • Accessibility audit achieving WCAG AA compliance.
  • Localization workflow integrated into Figma for quick content updates.

Outcomes

Business impact and user adoption

  • 50 % increase in active users within six months of launch (Spain).
  • Successful rollout to Italy, Germany, and Spain with a unified brand experience.
  • Reduced onboarding time by 30 % thanks to streamlined UI and in‑app tutorials.

Learnings

Balancing compliance with experience

Early collaboration with legal teams prevented re‑work. A modular design system enabled rapid localisation while keeping a consistent visual language.
