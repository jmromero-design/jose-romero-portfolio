Santander Nordics — CRO Programme

Conversion optimisation across four Nordic markets

Data‑driven UX improvements for lending, credit‑card and deposit funnels.

Ongoing programme since 2023, covering Denmark, Sweden, Finland and Norway, delivering measurable uplift in conversion rates.

Client: Santander Role: Lead UX/CRO Designer Period: 2023 — Present Scope: CRO · Banking · Multi‑market

Context

Multi‑market financial services conversion challenge

Santander sought to harmonise and improve conversion funnels across its Nordic digital banking products, each with local regulations and user expectations.

Challenge

Aligning data‑driven experimentation at scale

Coordinating A/B testing, analytics, and stakeholder alignment across four countries while maintaining brand consistency and regulatory compliance.

My Role

Strategic UX/CRO lead

Defined hypothesis library, designed experiments, and collaborated with product owners, engineers, and data teams to implement and analyse tests.

Approach

Iterative testing funnel

Implemented a tiered testing framework: quick micro‑tests on copy & UI, followed by larger flow‑level experiments, continuously feeding insights back to the product roadmap.

Execution

From hypothesis to rollout

  • Established a shared CRO dashboard for cross‑team visibility.
  • Designed responsive UI variations optimised for mobile‑first Nordic users.
  • Ensured WCAG AA accessibility across all test variants.

Outcomes

Significant conversion lift

  • Average conversion increase of 12% across core funnels.
  • Reduced cart abandonment by 8% through micro‑copy refinements.
  • Improved cross‑sell uptake for credit‑card offers by 15%.

Learnings

Balancing local nuance with global strategy

Success required respecting regional user behaviours while maintaining a unified optimisation process, highlighting the importance of adaptable test frameworks.
