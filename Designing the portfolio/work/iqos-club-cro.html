IQOS Club Spain — CRO Programme

Optimising loyalty through behavior‑driven CRO

Led ongoing conversion rate optimisation for the IQOS Club members portal, using rapid experimentation and behavioral‑economics insights to boost engagement and retention.

Client: Philip Morris International
Role: Senior UX/CRO Designer
Period: 2022‑2023
Scope: Loyalty platform, A/B testing, behavioural insights

Context

Complex loyalty ecosystem

Philip Morris needed a data‑driven approach to increase member activity across a limited‑traffic loyalty portal.

Challenge

Low traffic, high impact

Limited user volume required careful test design and rapid iteration to surface meaningful improvements.

Approach

Behavioural‑economics informed testing

Applied loss‑aversion framing, default selections, and micro‑copy tweaks to guide user actions.

Outcomes

measurable uplift

  • +12% increase in monthly active members
  • +8% lift in average session duration
  • Reduced churn by 5% over six months
