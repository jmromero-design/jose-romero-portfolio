About

Twelve years.
One consistent way of working.

I've been doing this since I was 21 — first running a small design studio in Havana, then building a career across Spain's largest enterprise accounts, and now embedded inside one of Europe's most recognised brands.

What's stayed constant across all of it: I work best when the environment is complex, the brief is incomplete, and the answer isn't obvious yet. I work in English and Spanish at professional level and operate comfortably across both.
